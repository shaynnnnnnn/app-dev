### 🔍 Synopsis

> During a total solar eclipse, a 25-year-old 21st-century woman, Go Ha-jin (Lee Ji-eun), is transported back to the Goryeo period.  
>  
> She wakes up in the year of 941, in the body of Hae Soo, among the many princes of the ruling Wang family, during the reign of King Taejo.  
>  
> She initially falls in love with the warm-hearted 8th Prince Wang Wook, but later develops a relationship with Wang So, the fearsome 4th Prince, labeled a "wolf-dog" by others.  
>  
> As the story unfolds, Hae Soo becomes entangled in palace politics and the brutal fight for the throne.

#### 💔 What Moved Me Most

> *What touched me the most was Hae Soo’s emotional strength.*

Despite her fears and countless struggles, she chooses to love Wang So wholeheartedly.

```text
"I wondered... will he follow her back to the future?"
```
It also leaves the viewer with a lingering sense of wonder and an unforgettable cliffhanger.

### 👥 Main Characters

- **Lee Joon-gi** as *4th Prince Wang So*  
  > Feared and misunderstood, he hides his face behind a mask and reveals his true self only to Hae Soo.

- **Lee Ji-eun** as *Go Ha-jin / Hae Soo*  
  > A 21st-century girl who travels to Goryeo. Kind-hearted and brave, she becomes a central figure in the lives of the princes.

- **Kang Ha-neul** as *8th Prince Wang Wook*  
  > Charming and loyal, but torn between love and ambition.
